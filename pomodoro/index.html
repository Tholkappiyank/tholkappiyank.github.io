    Help

    🕐 Clock

    Shows the current local time as a live flip clock. Theme can be toggled between dark and light using the sun icon in the header.

    🍅 Pomodoro Timer

    Work in focused intervals separated by short breaks.

    • Work / Short Break / Long Break tabs at the top switch session type.
    • Press ▶ to start, ⏸ to pause, ⏭ to skip.
    • Dots below the clock show progress through the current cycle.
    • Open Settings (⚙) to change durations, sound, and auto-start behaviour.
    • Open Tasks (☑) to track to-dos alongside your sessions.

    ⏱ Stopwatch

    Tap Start to begin. Tap Lap to record a split. Tap Reset to clear.

    ⏳ Countdown

    Set hours, minutes, and seconds then tap Start. A chime plays when time reaches zero. Tap Pause to hold, Reset to return to your set time.

    🔔 Alarm

    Enter a time and optional label then tap +. The alarm will trigger at that time with sound and a visual flash. Tap the trash icon to delete. Note: the page must remain open in your browser.
